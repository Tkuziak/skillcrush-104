Hi my name is Teresa and I have some advice for you!

Advice from a fellow coder

  1. 1.

    Don't worry if you feel lost when you start coding. It happens to everyone. It may take a while but all of a sudden, the coding will just click. Then you will be unstoppable!

  2. 2.

    Don't worry about new coding languages. They are just different ways to say the same thing. Once you feel comfortable coding in one language, other languages will be a breeze to learn!

  3. 3.

    Don't let people who use big words scare you. Most of the time, they don't actually know what they're talking about. Big technical words scare non-techies and make them think the techies know more than they do.
